About BudgetMate

BudgetMate is a personal finance management tool built specifically for New Zealanders. It helps users track their spending, set savings goals, and receive AI-powered advice on how to improve their financial health.

Key Features

Spend Pattern Analysis

Analyze your spending habits and discover areas where you can save more.

Smart Financial Advisor

Get personalized advice on budgeting, saving, and managing your finances.

Real-Time Financial Health Score

Monitor your financial health with an easy-to-understand score.

Custom Reports

Generate detailed financial reports to track your progress.

Data Insights

Using real-time data from Stats NZ, BudgetMate compares your spending, savings, and financial habits with the national averages. This gives you a clear perspective on how you can optimize your finances.
